Turok 2 review

summary

Turok 2 Seeds Of Evilis an Nintendo 64 game made by Akklaim studios This game has always been one of my favorites games for many reasons. I could go on about the amazing weapons you get throughout your adventure or the aray of enemies in your path, but I think this games shines most for how hard is it and the perfect soundtrack.

The main character is an indian named Jousha Fireseed who is summoned by an alien named Adon. She tells you world is going to end if you don't stop the Primeagon who is the main antagonist. You go out many different setting and worlds to collect primeagon keys to unlock the last level and defend energy beacons located in each level. Each level also has its own objectives that must be complete or else you will fail to beat the level adventure this game shows no mercy and will make you restart the whole thing.

The controlls feel very smooth for a game made in 1998 and the music in this game is dead on for what ever level you are on. The very first level drops you in a rundown taken over city and the song the plays is so catchy yet sounds almost frighting which gives you an erie feeling that gets your heart racing. I used to just sit on the pause screen and just listen to the song, thats how good it is. The enemies are mostley dinosoid mutants who can and will take 30 percent of your health very quick if you are not paying attention. The enemies are well placed throughout each level and each level is almost maze like with backtracking and worse of all no composs or anything telling you which way to go.

weaponary In Turok 2

This game has the coolest variety of weapons I think I have ever seen in a video game. You start off with the basics like a pistol and bow. As you go throughout your adventure you will find better and even more fun guns to use. My personal favorite is a circular blade which spins and then is thrown like a frisbee into enemies and somehow catches it on the return like a boomerang.

There are many other crazier and stronger weapons like a missle launcher with triple rockets in it. The most imfamous weapon in this game is the weapon called the cerboral bore. This locks onto only intelligent creatures and will drill into their head and then explode. To this day I have not seen anything as cool or as rememberable as this.
