The hat

Ever since I saw the Russia winter hat I had always had a lowkey passion to one day go there. I first saw a russian hat at my school and it was very cool and warm looking. Everyone else had normal boring hats and a few with strings and such in it, but nothing compared to this hat. I fell in love with it and I eventually got one. Mine was a brown fur outside with an orange inside and I still have the hat to this day. I got this hat at least 8 years ago.
